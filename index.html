Weather Conditions by Latitude

This project takes a look at four different aspects of the weather of cities around the globe and compares them against the latitude of the city.
The areas of data looked at are temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed. The data is plotted as points on a graph to see if there are any correlations.
