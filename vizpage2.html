Visualization: Latitude vs Humidity

On this dimension, the humidity was measured in percentage so the scale runs from 0 minimum to a maximum of 100. There doesn't appear to be a strong relationship between latitude and humidity. The data points are roughly scattered in the same manner from the negative to positive latitudes, with the exception of there being fewer 100% results below -20 degrees, suggesting it to be drier. This could be due to there being fewer overall results in the southern hemispher than the northern hemisphere.
